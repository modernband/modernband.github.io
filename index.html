Music is meant to be a social experience! The Modern Band Academy specializes in group instruction.

Private lessons will always have their place but we specialize in small group instruction. Our method gives students almost 2 hours every week of rehearsal time. (Compare that to only 30 minutes with private lessons.) Every student is at a different level and that's why we utilize the teaching method called "differentiation". For example, on a difficult song, young novice guitar players may opt to play the 3-string (E, B, G) guitar part while intermediate players will play full barre chords.

You will meet new friends! More importantly, as you learn to play the guitar, bass, drums, or keyboards, you will meet friends who like the same music as you do!

Available Classes and Pricing.

Our classes include the following instruments: electric guitar, electric bass, keyboards, drum set, and orchestral strings (violin, viola, cello, upright bass). At this time, we do not teach acoustic guitar or ukulele.

MBA tuition is $150 per month. For beginner classes (ages 10 to 17), there is a $30/month discount until you hit 1 year or until you wish to move to an intermediate class.

Hours of operation are from 3pm to 9pm, Monday thru Friday. 8am to Noon on Saturday. Classes available:

  1. Beginning Electric Guitar/Bass/Piano Ages 10-11
  2. Beginning Electric Guitar/Bass/Piano Ages 12-13
  3. Beginner Electric Guitar/Bass/Piano Ages 14-17
  4. Intermediate (all instruments) Ages 14-17
  5. Modern Strings Ages 12+

Classes that include the drum set only have 6pm and later on weekdays and prior to 10am on Saturday.
