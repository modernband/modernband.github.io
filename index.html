Music is more fun with other people! That's why we prefer group instruction.

Private lessons have their place but we specialize in small group instruction. Our method gives students almost 2 hours every week of rehearsal time. (Compare that to only 30 minutes with private lessons.) Every student is at a different level and that's why we have several parts for each song that range in difficulty.

Students learn the songs they love and know. Our curriculum is student-inspired and teacher-guided.

We have many songs in our curriculum that we know kids love. But we also want to listen to our students and let them express themselves with the music they listen to.
